Padding
  • The yellow (outter box) margin adds spacing from an element. In this case, the margin creates space from the red and blue paddings.
  • The red and blue (inner boxes) paddings creates space within the two elements. In this case, the word "padding" is centred and spaced from the borders.
  • The black outlines are called "borders". With borders, users are able to specify three things:
    1. Style - Solid
    2. Color - Black
    3. Width - 5px
